Eight books. Different subjects, different tones — but the same underlying preoccupations: clarity, proportion, and the work of living well. Browse below, and if something catches your attention, the cover image will take you to Amazon.

**On Proportion**
: A meditation on balance — moral, civic, and personal. The argument at its heart is simple but far-reaching: disorder in public life and private character begins when proportion is lost, and restoration begins with disciplined judgment, measured speech, and rightly ordered priorities. (Forthcoming)

**[Stoic Backgammon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0GXW6KRDH/)**
: Where the ancient game meets ancient philosophy. A practical exploration of how Stoic principles — accepting what the dice give you, focusing on what you control, and keeping your composure when fortune turns — apply both at the board and in life.

**[Letters](https://www.amazon.com/Letters-Without-Outcome-Philip-Huffman/dp/B0GLNWT4N7/)**
: A collection of unsent letters addressed to significant individuals. Some things are easier to say when you're not expecting a reply.

**[Misaligned](https://www.amazon.com/Misaligned-Subject-Adjective-Disastrous-Result/dp/B0GLX9JS3V/)**
: A memoir about how early experiences, loss, and misunderstanding can quietly shape a life — and how clarity sometimes arrives decades later than you'd like.

**[The Stoic CGM](https://www.amazon.com/Stoic-CGM-Data-Driven-Reinventing-Yourself/dp/B0G6G12GPW/)**
: A practical Stoic approach to using a continuous glucose monitor: observing without drama, running small experiments, making steady changes. Part data, part mindset — aimed at consistency rather than perfection.

**[The Stoic Citizen](https://www.amazon.com/Stoic-Citizen-Philip-Huffman-ebook/dp/B0G3LTQ8B6/)**
: Stoicism as a framework for living with judgment, dignity, and responsibility — in relationships, health, and public life. More reflective than prescriptive.

**[A Life Made Whole](https://www.amazon.com/Life-Made-Whole-Strength-Resilience-ebook/dp/B0G2GLKKCQ/)**
: An integration book for neurodivergent readers: how to bring the scattered parts of a life — work, health, relationships, meaning — into alignment. Less about hacks, more about acceptance and long-term direction.

**[Unstuck](https://www.amazon.com/Unstuck-Brutal-Guidance-Getting-Your-ebook/dp/B0DYL1G7HQ/)**
: Where it started. Direct, practical, and unsentimental — about the habits and narratives that keep us stuck, and how to change without pep talks or fluff.
