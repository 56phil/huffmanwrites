The 7-Day Credo Workshop is a practical, interactive program designed to help you apply the principles of "Think Clearly. Live Intentionally. Love Immediately." to your daily life. Through daily exercises, reflections, and guided practices, you'll develop the skills to integrate these principles into your decision-making, relationships, and personal growth.

## How It Works

The workshop consists of seven daily modules, each focusing on a specific aspect of the credo. You'll receive:
- **Daily video introduction** (5-10 minutes)
- **Guided exercises** to apply the principle
- **Reflection prompts** for journaling
- **Practical challenges** to integrate the concept
- **Community support** through discussion forums

Each day builds on the previous one, creating a comprehensive framework for intentional living.

## Workshop Outline

### Day 1: Think Clearly
**Focus**: Cognitive foundations and mental clarity
**Exercises**: Thought patterns, cognitive biases, decision frameworks
**Challenge**: Identify and reframe one habitual thought pattern

### Day 2: The Discipline of Attention
**Focus**: Focus and presence
**Exercises**: Attention training, mindfulness practices
**Challenge**: Practice focused attention for 10 minutes daily

### Day 3: Live Intentionally
**Focus**: Aligning actions with values
**Exercises**: Value identification, decision matrices
**Challenge**: Make one deliberate decision aligned with your values

### Day 4: The Practice of Proportion
**Focus**: Balancing priorities
**Exercises**: Time allocation, energy management
**Challenge**: Rebalance one area of your life

### Day 5: Love Immediately
**Focus**: Authentic connection
**Exercises**: Empathy practices, vulnerability exercises
**Challenge**: Create one moment of authentic connection

### Day 6: Integration
**Focus**: Combining all three principles
**Exercises**: Holistic application, scenario planning
**Challenge**: Apply the full credo to one complex situation

### Day 7: Commitment
**Focus**: Creating lasting change
**Exercises**: Habit formation, accountability planning
**Challenge**: Create a 30-day action plan
