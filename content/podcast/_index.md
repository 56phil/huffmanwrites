The Credo Podcast is an audio exploration of the principles "Think Clearly. Live Intentionally. Love Immediately." Each episode features conversations with philosophers, psychologists, scientists, and practitioners who help us understand how to apply these principles to modern life.

## About the Podcast

Hosted by Philip Huffman, The Credo Podcast goes beyond theory to practical application. Through in-depth interviews, solo reflections, and expert discussions, we explore:

- The cognitive science behind clear thinking
- The philosophy of deliberate action
- The psychology of authentic connection
- Practical strategies for intentional living
- Stories of transformation and growth

Each episode is designed to help you integrate the credo into your daily life, with actionable insights and thought-provoking discussions.

## Episode Guide

### Season 1: Foundations

**Episode 1: Think Clearly - The Cognitive Science of Decision Making**
*Guest: Dr. Sarah Chen, Cognitive Psychologist*
Explore the cognitive foundations of clear thinking, including common biases, decision frameworks, and strategies for improving mental clarity.

**Episode 2: Live Intentionally - Philosophy in Action**
*Guest: Dr. Marcus Aurelius Johnson, Philosopher*
Discuss the philosophical underpinnings of deliberate living, including Stoic principles, value alignment, and the discipline of action.

**Episode 3: Love Immediately - The Psychology of Connection**
*Guest: Dr. Elena Rodriguez, Social Psychologist*
Examine the science of authentic connection, including vulnerability, empathy, and the role of relationships in personal growth.

**Episode 4: Integration - Applying the Full Credo**
*Host: Philip Huffman*
A solo episode exploring how to combine all three principles in daily life, with practical examples and strategies for holistic application.

**Episode 5: The Stoic Approach to Modern Challenges**
*Guest: Ryan Holiday, Author of "The Daily Stoic"*
Discuss how Stoic principles align with the credo and can help navigate modern challenges with wisdom and resilience.
